Handmade leather wallets

In the workshop we make sturdy wallets from genuine leather. The handmade wallet differs from those sold in stores, a product of a completely different kind. The main difference is leather, its thickness is about 2 mm, due to which the product turns out to be weighty and reliable. The wallet is cut from the hide of cattle by hand, then we process the leather also by hand to get the desired look, then firmly glue and sew with a hand saddle stitch with a waxed thread in two needles.
